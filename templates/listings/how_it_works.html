Browse or List an Item

As a Renter: Search by category, location, or price. Filter by rating, availability, and more. Each listing shows real photos, pricing, and owner reviews.

Send a Rental Request

Choose your start and end dates. The system automatically calculates the total cost including the refundable security deposit. Add a message to the owner explaining your use case.

You are not charged until the owner approves your request.
4

Owner Reviews & Approves

The owner gets notified instantly. They review your profile and rental request. Once approved, you receive a confirmation notification. You can message the owner directly to arrange pickup or delivery.

Instant notification Direct messaging
5

Rent, Return & Review

Use the item for the agreed period. Return it on time. The owner marks it returned on the platform. Your security deposit is refunded. Both parties leave reviews — building a trusted community.

If an item is damaged during the rental period, the owner can raise a dispute through the platform. The security deposit helps cover repair costs. Always inspect items at pickup and document their condition.
